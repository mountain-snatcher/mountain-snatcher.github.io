About Euler Fluid Simulation

This simulation solves the compressible Euler equations using Taichi Lang with HLLC Riemann solver and MUSCL reconstruction. The visualization shows fluid dynamics in real-time with a vortex initial condition.

Features:

  • GPU-accelerated computation with Taichi
  • High-resolution finite volume method
  • Real-time WebSocket communication
  • Multiple visualization modes
